Privacy Policy

watcho  ·  Last updated: May 2026

Overview

watcho is a personal movie and TV show tracker for iOS and Android. All data you create — watchlists, ratings, and reviews — is stored locally on your device. No personal data is transmitted to servers operated by the developer.

Data we collect

We do not collect any personal information. We do not use analytics, crash reporting, advertising identifiers, or any other tracking technology.

Third-party services

watcho connects to the following external services on your behalf:

  • The Movie Database (TMDB) — used to fetch movie and TV show metadata (titles, descriptions, posters, trailers, streaming availability). Your requests to TMDB include search terms and title IDs you look up; no personally identifiable information is included. Your TMDB API key is stored locally on your device and sent only to TMDB's servers.
    TMDB Privacy Policy
  • YouTube (via embedded player) — trailers are played through an embedded YouTube WebView. YouTube's own privacy policy applies when a video is played.
    Google / YouTube Privacy Policy

Your data

All watchlist entries, ratings, and reviews are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data is never transmitted to the developer or any third party. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

TMDB API key

You provide your own TMDB API key during the initial setup. This key is stored locally on your device and sent only to TMDB's API servers to load content. It is never shared with the developer or any other party.

Children's privacy

watcho is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised date.
