AGE RATING QUESTIONNAIRE
========================
App Store Connect → App Information → Age Rating
Answer each question as follows:


VIOLENCE
  Cartoon or Fantasy Violence:               NONE
  Realistic Violence:                        INFREQUENT/MILD
  Prolonged Graphic or Sadistic Violence:    NONE

SEXUAL CONTENT
  Sexual Content or Nudity:                  NONE
  Graphic Sexual Content and Nudity:         NONE

OTHER
  Profanity or Crude Humor:                  NONE
  Mature / Suggestive Themes:                INFREQUENT/MILD
  Horror / Fear Themes:                      INFREQUENT/MILD
  Medical / Treatment Information:           NONE
  Alcohol, Tobacco, or Drug Use:             NONE
  Simulated Gambling:                        NONE
  Unrestricted Web Access:                   NO
  Made for Kids (COPPA):                     NO

EXPECTED RESULT: 9+

RATIONALE
---------
watcho is a personal tracker app. It contains no user-generated content and
no objectionable content of its own. However, users can search for any movie
or TV show in the TMDB catalogue and play its trailer via an embedded YouTube
WebView. This means trailers for horror films, R-rated thrillers, and movies
with mature or suggestive themes are reachable through normal app usage.

The three "Infrequent/Mild" answers reflect that this content is incidental
(trailers, not full films) and user-initiated (the user searches for the
title), but still accessible. Graphic Sexual Content stays NONE because
YouTube age-gates explicit content itself. "Unrestricted Web Access" remains
NO — the embedded player is not a free-roam browser.
