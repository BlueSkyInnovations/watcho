DESCRIPTION  [~1 900 / 4 000 chars]
-----------
watcho is a personal movie and TV show tracker that lives entirely on your device. No account required. No ads. No subscription.

Keep track of everything you want to watch, what you are currently watching, and every title you have already seen — across both movies and TV shows.

THREE SIMPLE LISTS
• Watchlist — titles you plan to watch
• Watching — what you are in the middle of right now
• Watched — everything you have already seen

Moving a title between lists is a single tap.

RICH DETAIL SCREENS
Open any movie or TV show to see its backdrop, poster, genres, overview, and TMDB community rating — all fetched in your chosen language. Add your own personal star rating and a private text review alongside.

WATCH TRAILERS
Play trailers directly in the app. watcho filters by language and lets you browse by season for TV shows.

WHERE TO WATCH
See streaming, rental, and purchase options for your region, powered by JustWatch data via TMDB.

PROGRESS TRACKING
Follow TV shows season by season, episode by episode. Advance your counter with a single tap.

DISCOVER MORE
Every title page includes a row of recommendations. Search any movie or TV show by name — results appear as you type.

YOUR STATS
The Profile tab shows your total watched count, a movie vs. TV show breakdown, your average star rating, and your top genres.

CUSTOMISE YOUR EXPERIENCE
• Light, Dark, or System theme
• App language: English, German, Spanish, and more via community translation
• Toggle individual detail screen sections on or off
• Sort each list by Date Added, Release Date, or Last Updated
• Switch between grid and list view

IOS QUICK ACTIONS
Long-press the app icon to jump to your current show or advance its episode counter — without even opening the app.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Everything stays on your device. No server, no sync, no cloud. Your lists, ratings, and reviews are stored locally and never leave your phone.

Content data is provided by The Movie Database (TMDB), a free community-maintained database. A free personal TMDB API key is required — the app walks you through getting one on first launch.
